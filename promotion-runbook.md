## Main Angle

Lead with the real story:

I am a Sudoku fan. Normal 1-9 Sudoku can become abstract and difficult, so I wondered whether Chinese Mahjong tiles could make the puzzle more visual, more challenging, and more interesting for both Mahjong players and Sudoku players.

## Product Hunt

Frame it as a simple puzzle experiment:

Classic single-solution Sudoku, but with Mahjong-inspired tile suits instead of normal digits.

Ask:
Does the tile visual language make the puzzle feel fresher, or do numbers remain easier to solve?

## Indie Hackers

Use the builder angle:

I built a small puzzle app from my own Sudoku habit and tried to combine it with Mahjong visual language.

Good question:
How do you decide when a puzzle variation is meaningful enough to become its own app?

## Reddit

Do not post immediately from a new account if the previous Reddit post was filtered.

Later, use a soft feedback title:

Feedback wanted: Sudoku with Mahjong tile symbols

Avoid multiple links. Use either the founder story or the App Store link, not both.
